Preparing for Hurricane Irma

Remember:

  • Customers’ voicemail boxes are protected within Frontier’s network and will continue to operate despite a power loss.
  • Your safety is our priority. Stay far away from downed cables or power lines.
  • In the event of a commercial power outage, corded phones will still operate. If commercial power is unavailable, generators and batteries in Frontier’s central offices serve as backup. Phone lines generally will have enough power in them to use a corded phone.
